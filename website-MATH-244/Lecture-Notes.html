Chapter 12

The material we will learn in the main chapters is based on a good knowledge of the material in Chapter 12. It explains how to represent a point in the 3D space and many useful formulas are presented (vectors, dot product, equations of many popular surfaces, etc.).

Here are the sections required

  • For Chapter 15:
    • Section 12.1
    • Section 12.5
    • Section 15.6
  • For Chapter 16:
    • Section 12.2
    • Section 12.3
    • Section 12.4
You may practice with some of the exercises from these sections. Try the odd numbered exercises at the end of each section. In this way, you can check your answer with the answer key in Appendix H of the textbook.
